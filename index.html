Heroes of World War I

Private 11945
Frederick Spearink

2nd Battalion Hampshire Regiment
Drowned at sea on the HMT Royal Edward, 13th August 1915, aged 33.
Private Spearink is commemorated at the Mitcham Memorial and Helles Memorial.
He left behind his Wife Mrs. Sarah Ellen Spearink and their six children who lived in Streatham, South London.
